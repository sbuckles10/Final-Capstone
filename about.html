About the Poutine Factory

California has many different types of food, however, Canadian food is not well represented. Our solution is a restaurant called the Poutine Factory. The Poutine Factory is a family-style restaurant with a Canadian theme. Our main dish will be poutine, which is an iconic Canadian dish. It consists of fries topped with gravy and cheese curds. We will also serve other dishes as well, but with a focus on poutine and other Canadian dishes such as split pea soup, Hawaiian pizza and beavertails.
